From Freeways to Greenways

Originally Published: July 23, 2018 | Updated for 2026 Sustainable Initiatives

The transformation of urban infrastructure from concrete-heavy freeways to lush, carbon-absorbing greenways is more than an aesthetic choice—it's a necessity for future-proof cities.

Why Greenways Matter for Solar Communities:

  • Reduced Urban Heat Island: Lower ambient temperatures increase the efficiency of local solar arrays.
  • Smart Infrastructure: Integrating solar-powered lighting and charging stations along green corridors.
  • Community Health: Bridging the gap between energy production and local recreation.

At Local Sun Energy, we believe that the same philosophy that powers our greenways should power our homes. Transitioning to renewable energy is the final piece of the urban sustainability puzzle.
